Leadership

Some dogs

Some dogs are friendly, intelligent, and devoted companions. They are excellent family pets renowned for their gentle nature with children.

Originally bred for retrieving waterfowl, they have a natural love for water and outdoor activities.

Dogs require regular exercise and mental stimulation to stay happy and healthy.

Most dogs live 10-12 years and are generally robust.

They excel in obedience, agility, and even therapy work.

Other dogs

Other dogs are highly intelligent and energetic working breeds originally developed to herd sheep. They thrive on mental challenges and vigorous exercise.

Known for intense focus and problem-solving skills, they dominate dog sports and advanced training courses.

Good dogs

Good dogs are:

  • Excellent family companions
  • Great with children and other pets
  • Highly trainable and eager to please
  • In need of daily exercise and mental stimulation

Most dogs

Most dogs are confident, courageous, and versatile working animals that form strong bonds with their families.

Though originally herders, many now excel in police work, military service, and search-and-rescue roles.

Their natural protectiveness also makes them superb watchdogs.

About Al Shirawi Group
