About Al Shirawi Group

Oasis Investment Company LLC (OIC), the holding company of the Al Shirawi Group of Companies, was founded in Dubai in 1971, by two visionary leaders – Abdulla Al Shirawi and Mohan Valrani. From its humble beginnings as a trading and contracting company, the Group has grown into one of the largest private business conglomerat.
